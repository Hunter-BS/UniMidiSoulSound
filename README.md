# UniMidiSoulSound
A soundfont made from genuine artists and musicians, not sample libraries, permanently free. Public Domain.

## _Warning!_
When any samples are submitted, the rights to the samples are immediately absolved (i.e. forfieted) simply by design of the project. You can contact for removal if you wish for them removed before the next release, but once the next release is published, the previous release will not be removed, due to the license.

## Importing Samples
Check the GM Standard to see which group your instrument is in!
Use the appropriate folders, and either drop a full recording as FULL_\[identifier].[file type] or split samples in a new folder [identifier] (or) [instrument]. Alternatively, a Google Drive link may be provided as a drop location.
